Rules: The object of the game is to become the wealthiest player through, 
buying,renting and selling of property.

Money: Each player is given $1500 to start with.

The Play: After you have completed your play. Two or more players may rest on
the same space at the same time.Depending on the space you may be entitled to 
buy property, or be obliged to pay rent, pay taxes, draw a Chance or Community 
Chest card, Go To Jail, or etc..

Go: Each time a player passes Go the player receives $200.

Buying Property: Whenever you land on an unowned property you may buy that 
property from the Bank at its printed price.

Paying Rent: When you land on a property that is owned by another player, 
the owner collects rent from you in accordance with the rent cost.

Chance & Community Chest: When you land on either of these spaces, take the 
top card from the deck indicated, follow the instructions.

Income Tax: If you land here you must pay $200 to the bank.

Jail: You land in jail when you move to the space marked "Go to Jail", if 
you land on jail and are not in jail then you are "just visiting".

Free Parking: A player landing on this place does not receive any money, 
property or reward of any kind. This is just a "free" resting-place.

Bankruptcy: When you are declared bankrupt, if you owe more than you can 
pay either to another player or to the bank. You must retire from the game.
